## Examples:

 **Anyone But You** - **Rise**: Meet at wedding and hook up вҶ’ **Fall**: Big fight and humiliation вҶ’ **Rise Higher**: Realize they actually love each other

- **Mamma Mia!** - **Rise**: Sam arrives after 20 years, old chemistry returns вҶ’ **Fall**: Painful memories resurface, resistance and anger вҶ’ **Rise Higher**: Reconcile at wedding, finally together

- **Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse** - **Rise**: Miles reconnects with Gwen, team-up goes great вҶ’ **Fall**: Miguel and Spider-Society turn against him, Gwen betrays him вҶ’ **Rise Higher**: Gwen's team rescues him, stronger bond formed
